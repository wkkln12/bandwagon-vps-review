## Who Is Bandwagon Host (BandwagonHost)?

Bandwagon Host, officially called **BandwagonHost**, is a VPS hosting brand operated by IT7 Networks Inc., a Canadian company that's been in the infrastructure business since 2012. The service runs on **KVM virtualization** with its proprietary **KiwiVM control panel** — a clean, no-nonsense panel where you can start/stop your server, reload your OS, migrate data centers, take snapshots, manage rDNS records, and access usage stats.

- **21+ data centers** across the US, Canada, Japan, Hong Kong, Tokyo, Osaka, Amsterdam, and the UAE.
- **99.9%–99.99% uptime guarantees** (depending on plan tier).
- **30-day money-back policy** on most plans.

## Things to Know Before You Buy

**It's self-managed.** Bandwagon Host gives you a server and gets out of the way. The KiwiVM panel handles the basics cleanly, but if you need help debugging your WordPress install, that's on you. Support handles infrastructure and network issues, not application-level stuff.

**DDoS protection has limits.** The CN2 GIA network handles DDoS attacks via IP nullrouting, which can cause temporary downtime during an attack. If you expect heavy DDoS exposure, consider whether this works for your use case.

**Datacenter migration is genuinely useful.** You can migrate your VPS between supported data centers directly from KiwiVM without data loss — no need to reprovision. This is actually rare and saves a lot of headaches if you want to test performance from different locations.

**Annual billing is the smart move.** Paying monthly costs significantly more. The annual rate on the PROMO VPS is $49.99 — the monthly rate for comparable plans is $19.99 (for the 80G tier). Run the math before choosing your billing cycle.
